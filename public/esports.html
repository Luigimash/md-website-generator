I've been working in esports since 2019, when I started an esports club at my highschool. Freelancing, observing, producing, collegiate, and leading students are all in my game.

Working and playing games, competitively and casually, has always been a big part of my life. Without it, I don't know who I would be today.

While I'm not planning to actively pursue a career in esports anymore, the community still has a place near and dear to my heart. You can find me attending events and cheering on my friends in the industry.

2021 - 2024
